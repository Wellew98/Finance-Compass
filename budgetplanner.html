Smart Budgeting Tips

Follow these strategies to make the most of your budget and improve your financial health.

50/30/20 Rule

Allocate 50% of your income to needs, 30% to wants, and 20% to savings and debt repayment for a balanced budget approach.

Emergency Fund Priority

Build an emergency fund that covers 3-6 months of expenses before focusing on other financial goals.
